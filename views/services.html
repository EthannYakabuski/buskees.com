Buskees Services: Ottawa

Lawn Mowing

Lawn Mowing is the bulk of Buskees work, we intend to provide low cost service so that we can continue to serve you and your lawn weekly, bi-weekly, monthly or as needed basis for the rest of the summer months

  • Tiny Lawn: $10
  • Small Lawn: $15
  • Medium Lawn: $20
  • Large Lawn: $25
  • Huge Lawn: $30

A tiny lawn is that of a townhouse with a small backyard and front yard

A small lawn is that of condo or townhouse with a backyard, front yard and some on the sides

A medium lawn is that of a regular sized city home with a frontyard, backyard and some to the sides

A large lawn is that of a family home with bigger then normal frontyard and backyard with some to the sides

A huge yard is that of a family home found in the outer parts of the city or the countryside, with lots of green space

General Labor

General Labor, whatever it may be is provided at 16/hour of labor provided. This can be anything around the house such as; digging holes, lifting heavy things, laying stone/interlock, laying sod, raking leaves and general summer/spring cleaning, washing vehicles and cleaning driveways. If you are in need of any other services please see our contact page and inquire.

Driveway Sealing

These jobs are provided at an as need basis; our field agents are not equipped with the tar on them, but they can schedule a time (weather permitting) for someone to come back and service your driveway within our hours of operation. No funds would be collected until you have seen a finished driveway of course! That is how Buskees operates.

The cost of these jobs vary depending on amount of crack filler and tar needed to do a good job. If you haven't already received a quote please see our contact page to use your preferred method of communication and we can quote your property within 12 hours.
